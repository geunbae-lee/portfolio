GT FindClass Gundam

Overview

Description

Introducing the all-new Georgia Tech app, completely rewritten from the ground up! Your guide to everything you’ll need while on campus.

PLACES
• View detailed information for all buildings on campus, including operating hours and nearby bus stops
• Find a place to eat or easily figure out where your classroom building is located

EVENTS
• Keep track of what’s happening on campus

BUSES
• View up-to-date locations and time predictions for Georgia Tech Stinger buses and Tech Trolley.

NEWS
• Stay up-to-date with the latest news in the GT community

ORGANIZATIONS
• Explore the many clubs & student organizations GT has to offer

UNIVERSAL SEARCH
• Search news articles, places, bus stops, organizations, and sites all in one place
• Accessible (almost) anywhere in the app

+ AND MUCH, MUCH MORE!
