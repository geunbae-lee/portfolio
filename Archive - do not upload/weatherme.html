What it's about

Weather Me is a weather app which may seem similar to the ones currently out there in the world. But, what's different about Weather Me is that it allows the users to "feel" the current weather based memory.

Imagine a situation where you have to open a weather app to check the weather for today at 12PM. On your weather app, it says, 24 degrees Celcius which is all you can get from it. You will not remember what 24 degrees Celcius "feels" like. However, weather app calculates the difference in temperature from yesterday and provide you the information. This way, it allows the users to think back in their memories of what they were doing at 12PM yesterday, what they were wearing and where they were.
